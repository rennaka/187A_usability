10. Help and Documentation

"Even though it is better if the system can be used without documentation, it may be necessary to provide help and documentation. Any such information should be easy to search, focused on the user's task, list concrete steps to be carried out, and not be too large".

When interacting with the schedule of classes, it is hard to figure out what classes were available and which were not.

The first attempt clicked-through to the page on the right, which at first glance was not very visible on how to attempt the search again.

We reached a conclusion that this schedule of classes was rather dated. We thought it would be cool to interact with something a little bit more dynamic.

The good point here is that we can do specific search in function of different criteria. A documentation to help users is available.

But two main things are bad. The first thing is that we enter our search term and then choose our specific criterion. It's not a logical way because our specific criterion is dependant on our search term.The second point is that the layout is bad.
