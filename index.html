Welcome to our site usability evaluation of Louisiana University's website

The two main goals of this project are, on the one hand, to criticize the Louisiana University's website using the Nielsen's 10 Heuristics, and on the other hand, to create a website showing our work using screenshots.

So, in order to be efficient each member of the team of 3, had to perform his private evaluation. After that we were able to create a common power point, which will use to create the gallery in our website. The last step consisted in the creation of a website's sketch and the realization of it.

To facilitate the navigation, we decided to use a different color for each criterion. Moreover, for each page you will have the images on the right and the explicative texts on the left. The pictures will show up in a gallery. You will find also a specific page, called Nielsen's 10 usability heuristics, describing the 10 criterions.
